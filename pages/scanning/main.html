ROC / Hand-Mining Gems (Multi-tool / ROC / ATLS GEO)

Very low signatures compared to ship rocks. Use low-power scan (25–50%) from ROC or close range. Most common single rock / small cluster values reported by community.
Gem Typical Single Rock RS Large / Cluster Example Best Moons / Notes
Hadanite ~120–150 ~600–620 (large) Aberdeen, Daymar, Arial – pink crystals, highest value
Dolivine ~120–150 ~600–620 Cellin, Yela, microTech moons – green
Aphorite ~120 ~600–620 Caves & surface outcrops – violet
Janalite ~120 ~600–620 Magda, Ita, Wala
Quick ROC Tips
  • 120 ≈ single small gem, 240 ≈ 2, 600–620 ≈ large / good cluster
  • Scan low & slow – large planetary rocks can hide small ones if power is too high
  • Hadanite sells raw (~270–300 aUEC/unit) – aim for clusters > 3–4 rocks
  • Best spots in 4.x: Aberdeen (Hadanite heavy), Daymar caves
  • If rocks don't interact → relog or server hop (common bug)
